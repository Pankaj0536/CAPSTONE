Operating System Concepts

Disk Access Time Model

Access Time = Seek Time + Rotational Latency + Transfer Time

HDD: Performance heavily depends on moving the mechanical disk head. Scheduling algorithms aim to minimize Seek Time.

SSD: No moving parts! Access time is almost constant. Scheduling is mostly irrelevant for SSDs, which is why SSD times in our simulation scale linearly and identically regardless of the algorithm.
